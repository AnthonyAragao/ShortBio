SHORT BIO

Ortenilton has a degree in Mathematics from the Federal University of Sergipe (UFS). As an undergraduate student, he participated three times in the Institutional Program for Scientific Initiation Scholarships (PIBIC), it's a program aimed at the development of scientific thinking and initiation into research by higher education undergraduate students; he participated in the Institutional Scholarship Program for Teaching Initiation (PIBID), it's an initiative whose purpose is to promote initiation into teaching, confident of improving the training of teachers at a higher level and improving the quality of public basic education in Brazil. He is currently a master's student in Pure Mathematics at the Pontifical Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro (PUC-Rio) under the joint supervision of Gustavo Araújo and Edgard A. Pimentel, funded by a high selective schema (FAPERJ Nota 10) and is interested in the area of analysis and partial differential equations, with emphasis on regularity theory for viscosity solutions in the domain of fully nonlinear models.
